Character Creation

ATTRIBUTES

In the game there are 9 attributes. These attributes are Strength,Toughness, and Nimbleness. These are the physical attributes. Luck, Charisma and Command are known as the social attributes. And Wits, Intelligence and willpower are the intellectual attributes.

  • Strength: The brute force and the application thereof.
  • Toughness: When the world knocks you around, it is this that keeps you standing.
  • Nimbleness: If you have the desire to put your legs behind your head this is for you.

  • Luck: Sometimes it is better to be lucky then good.
  • Charisma: Be it beauty or a pleasant personality.
  • Command: Some people just have that ability to lead.

  • Willpower: Stubbornness can take you farther then you otherwise might have gotten.
  • Intelligence: The pure logic power of the brain.
  • Wits: Your brain might wear the equivalent of clogs or running shoes.

All these attributes will have a value between 0 and 5, where the higher the number the better your attribute is. The average attribute of a human is 2.

  1. Intelligence: You are even dumber then a brick. The fact that you do things is a constant source of amazement to everyone.
  2. Nimbleness: If you really make an effort you might bend down far enough that you can see how someone could possibly reach their toes.
  3. Strength: Pretty average. Carrying a moving box of books is possible but tiring.
  4. Charisma: You have no trouble making friends.
  5. Willpower: Marathon runners and long distance racers.
  6. Wits: Sometimes you answer questions before they are even thought out.

Secondary attributes:

Health: Health always start at a value of 10, although some perks or flaws may alter that. Your characters health may raise and fall during the course of the game due to injuries and healing

Skills:

Like attributes, skills are in values from 0 to 5. Some of the kills can be used even with a 0 value and they are called Basic skills. Opposite those are the Advanced skills that can not be used with no value. They simply require training in them to be of any use.

Strength based skills:
Brawl: (Basic)
Fighting with only your body and or small improvised weapons like rocks.
Double handed melee weapons: (Basic)
Fighting with a large double handed sword, club or something similar.
Double handed ranged weapons: (Basic)
Using a large gun or equivalent.
Toughness based skills:
Survival: (Basic)
Finding the means to survive. Includes finding eadible plants, bugs, water and basic shelter from the elements.
Athletics: (Basic)
Moving around in various ways on, in, or through varrious terrain features.
Water vehicles: (Advanced)
To guide, navigate and drive vehicles.
Nimbleness based skills:
One handed melee weapons: (Basic)
Fighting with a smaller melee weapons. Daggers, swords, axes and other nasty implements of harm.
One handed ranged weapons: (Basic)
Pistols and similar fall into this category.
Dodge: (Basic)
Avoiding physical harm by not being in the same place as something harmful.
Command based skills:
Lead: (Advanced)
To lead people in an efficient manner. Also to convince larger amounts of people of doing things your way.
Persuade: (Basic)
The skill to make people see things your way.
Animal Care: (Advanced)>/dt>
To train and care for animals.
Intelligence based skills:
Herbs: (Advanced)
This skill allows you to find and use herbs. Herbs can be used to make potions and poisons.
Knowledge ____: (Advanced)
This is the skill of knowing something out of the ordernairy. This skill has to be specialized.
Wits based skills:
Fast talk: (Basic)
Use this skill to distract and confuse people.
Track: (Advanced)
To follow tracks left by people and creatures.
Stealth: (Basic)
This skill is pretty obvious. The use if it is not if done correctly.
Charisma based skills:
Lie: (Basic)
Lying and deceiving people around you.
Seduce: (Basic)
Using your wiles to make people do your bidding.
Empathy: (Basic)
Judging others emotions and sincerity.
Willpower based skills:
Resistance: (Basic)
This skill is used to resist the elements and above normal stress factors. Like continuing to function despite high levels of pain or discomfort.
Art & preform____: (Basic)
This skill is used to make are or preform by for example dancing or singing. This skill has to be specialized.
Awareness: (Basic)
Spot things with this skill.

Character Points:

When you create a character you have 100 points to spend on attributes, skills and traits. Additional points can be gained by some perks that are less advantageous.
